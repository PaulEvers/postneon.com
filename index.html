Post Neon, based in Amsterdam, is a brand new Multi Media Design and Production studio formed by Jim Brady and Vito Boeckx. We explore alternate realities, physical and non physical space. The studio tackles complex problems from contemporary culture by breaking them into new, consumable forms.

We have worked with several institutions like MU Artspace, TU/E Innovation Space and brands such as Adidas. Dezeen mentioned one of our projects as best graduation projects of 2018 and WGSN(Insider) featured our work in their report that unpacks the ten most influential concepts for 2019.
Logo by Ward Goes
Serif Babe typeface by Charlotte Rohde
Web design and development by Big Mistqke
